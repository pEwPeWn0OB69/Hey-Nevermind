Elon Musk

Elon Musk, (born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa), South African-born American entrepreneur who cofounded the electronic-payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX, maker of launch vehicles and spacecraft. Founded: SpaceX, PayPal, Zip2, X.com, Tesla, Inc Organization: SpaceX Born: June 28, 1971, Pretoria Profession: Entrepreneur, Investor
